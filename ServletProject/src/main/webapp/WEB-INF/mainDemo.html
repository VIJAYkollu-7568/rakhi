Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish is simple to make and incredibly delicious. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a cozy weekend meal.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta and quickly toss it with the pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve hot.
